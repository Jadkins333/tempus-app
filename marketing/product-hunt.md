## First Comment from Maker

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I built Tempus because I wanted a Dale-like year progress tracker that works everywhere — not just iOS. The concept is simple: 365 dots, one for each day. Watch them fill in. Feel time move.

I added event countdowns because knowing "47 days until vacation" keeps me going through the boring weeks.

**A few things I'm proud of:**
- It's fast. The whole app is under 50KB.
- It works offline. Install it on your phone and it feels native.
- It's private. Zero data leaves your device.
- No account required. Just open the URL and go.

The free tier gives you 2 countdowns and the default theme. The $2.99 one-time premium unlocks 9 color themes and unlimited countdowns. No subscriptions, no ads, no tracking.

I'd love your feedback on the UX and what features you'd want next. Thinking about weekly/monthly progress views, shared countdowns, and widget support.

Try it: https://jadkins333.github.io/tempus-app/

## 3 Key Features

### 🟣 365-Dot Year Progress Grid
See your entire year at a glance. Each dot = one day. Filled = past. Glowing = today. Empty = ahead. Know exactly where you stand.

### ⏱ Event Countdowns
Add dates that matter to you and see a beautiful countdown card with days remaining. Vacation, deadlines, birthdays — always know what's coming.

### 📱 Install Anywhere (PWA)
Works on any device with a browser. Add to your home screen for a native app experience. Works offline. No app store needed.
